Linen Wash Care Guide

1. Pre-Wash Tips

  • Separate linen from other fabrics, especially dark or heavy items, to avoid pilling or damage.
  • Check for stains and spot-treat them gently with mild soap or detergent.

2. Washing Linen

  • Machine Wash: Use a gentle cycle with lukewarm or cold water. Avoid hot water to prevent shrinking.
  • Hand Wash: Use mild detergent and lukewarm water. Gently agitate the fabric without wringing or scrubbing.

3. Detergents

  • Choose a mild, eco-friendly detergent free of harsh chemicals or bleach to maintain the natural fibers.

4. Drying

  • Air Dry: Lay flat or hang to dry in a shaded, well-ventilated area to avoid sun damage.
  • Tumble Dry: Use a low heat setting, but avoid over-drying to preserve softness.

5. Ironing

  • For a crisp look, iron linen while itâ€™s still slightly damp using medium heat.
  • For a relaxed, natural look, leave wrinkles as part of linen's charm.

6. Storing Linen

  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent discoloration.
  • Avoid plastic bags; use breathable fabric covers to allow airflow.

7. Extra Tips

  • Over time, linen becomes softer with each wash.
  • Avoid overloading the machine to reduce creases.
