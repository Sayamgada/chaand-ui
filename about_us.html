About Us

Welcome to CHAAND

Where timeless elegance meets sustainable fashion.
Inspired by the legacy of my beloved grandfather, Panachand, CHAAND carries the blessings of his name and the vision to create a brighter, conscious future. Rooted in authenticity and guided by our commitment to sustainability, CHAAND celebrates the natural beauty of linen — a fabric as enduring and versatile as the moon that lights up our name.

Our Philosophy

At CHAAND, we believe that fashion should not only make you look good but also feel good — about what you wear and how it’s made. That’s why our collections are crafted with:

  • Pure Linen: A breathable, durable, and biodegradable fabric that gets softer with every wear.
  • Sustainable Practices: From ethical sourcing to eco-friendly packaging, every step of our process is designed to minimize our environmental impact.

Our Mission

To become a leading purveyor of premium linen clothing, inspiring individuals to make stylish, conscious choices. We aim to harmonize fashion and sustainability, proving that elegance can coexist with responsibility.

Our Collection

Explore a world of thoughtfully designed co-ord sets, dresses, and more, each piece crafted to embody understated luxury and effortless charm. Whether you’re dressing for work, leisure, or a special moment, CHAAND ensures you’ll shine in comfort and style.

Why Choose CHAAND?

  • Timeless Designs: Made to last beyond trends, offering versatility for any occasion.
  • Ethical Values: Supporting mindful living and a greener planet.
  • Personal Connection: A brand born from heritage, love, and care.

Join Us

Celebrate slow fashion, embrace natural beauty, and wear your values proudly.
