Collection Padma

The name Padma means lotus, a flower that represents purity, resilience, and beauty. This collection is deeply inspired by those values – crafted with pure, breathable linen, designed to bring elegance and comfort to your everyday life. At CHAAND, sustainability is at the heart of everything we do. With Padma, we’ve used 100% natural, biodegradable fabrics, dyed with eco-conscious methods to ensure a minimal impact on the planet. Each piece is thoughtfully designed to celebrate simplicity and versatility. Whether you’re stepping out for a casual day or dressing up for an occasion, the Padma Collection has something timeless for everyone.
